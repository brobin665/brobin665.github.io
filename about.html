Hello. My name is Brian Robinson, and I am a geographer and web and digital media developer. I was raised in rural Southwest Wisconsin, and I currently reside in central Wisconsin.

I attended college at University of Wisconsin Stevens Point, where I graduated with a Bachelor's Degree of Science with double majors in Geography Information Systems and Cartography, and also Web and Digital Media Development. I am currently attending the University of Wisconsin Madison, where I intend to graduate with a Master's Degree of Science from the Online Professional Master's GIS and Web Map Programming department. I am seeking employment in the fields of GIS and Web Development. Cartography has always been of great interest to me, and I am eager to learn and see how technology will continue to advance modern cartography.

When I'm not learning about the new and latest technologies of GIS and web design, I spend my time with family and friends doing a variety of activities. I am an avid Chicago Bears fan, and enjoy watching as many games as my schedule allows. I do enjoy the occasional video game binge, but try to spend as much time outdoors as the Wisconsin weather will allow.

I have maintained several part-time and seasonal jobs since the age of 15, and I am excited to begin my career in the fields of cartography and web development. Explore the rest of this website to find projects I have completed, and to stay updated on my current projects.
